About

Midnight Sun is an indie rock band, that combines the rythmic surf rock vibes with dream pop and shoegaze sounds that creates the band unique dream rock genre.
The unique sound is created by two guitars, one guitar plays the rythmic surf rock vibes, while the second guitar adds the dreamy and melodical riffs together with rock n'roll drums beat and a bass groove who's most likely to be found in EDM music.
Midnight Sun is a four group members from israel who write and produce their own music all by themselfs from their home studio.
